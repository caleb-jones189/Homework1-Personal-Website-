Anime

My favorite anime is Death Note

While it is very close, Death Note is my favorite anime. The main character is Light Yagmi, the smartest student at his high school. He walks apon a book that gives him the power to kill people. If feels that he is doing the world a good service because he is getting rid of evil people. But that raises the question, if we are given the power to kill someone, are we morally obligated to do so? This show addresses that question in great detail. It has also made me question capital punishment in the United States.
